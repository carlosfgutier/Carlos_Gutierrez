Philosophy

I am a developer and a researcher. Web development and research demand many of the same skills, and both are essential when creating a great product. The best user experience comes from a strong understanding of the needs of the user and code that does what it is intended to do. Good practices are the result of learning and experimenting over time, isolating variables, and identifying what works. Human behavior and technology progressively become more intertwined, understanding both is the key to creating tools that make life better and keep us engaged.
